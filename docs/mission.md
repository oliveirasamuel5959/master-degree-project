# Mission

## Project Name

Urban Threat Anticipation using Computer Vision and Temporal Deep Learning

---

## Vision

Develop an intelligent surveillance system capable of identifying behavioral patterns that precede criminal activities in urban environments and generating early warnings before the crime occurs.

Unlike traditional surveillance systems that detect crimes after they happen, this project focuses on anticipation and risk estimation.

---

## Main Research Question

Can criminal activities be anticipated from human behavioral patterns observed in surveillance videos before the criminal event actually occurs?

---

## Scientific Hypothesis

Criminal actions are preceded by observable behavioral patterns.

These patterns can be learned through:

- Human trajectory analysis
- Human interaction modeling
- Temporal sequence modeling
- Attention-based learning
